Software development is the closest thing we have to magic. We put energy into rocks, and with enough time, focus and esoteric symbols to control that energy, those rocks turn our ideas into reality. We have the power to build tools that solve almost any problem, share knowledge across the planet in an instant, and utilize data-driven approaches to making lives better.

At the same time, writing software today comes with real responsibility. We’re building in a world where technology is routinely used for theft, propaganda, data-hoarding, and quiet forms of manipulation. Security in such a world is non-negotiable.

I design systems that collect the minimum data needed, store it safely, and give users real control over how it’s used. Security isn’t just about patching vulnerabilities, it’s about respecting boundaries. I assume that everything I build may someday be attacked or misused and I plan accordingly.

I ship software that’s not only fast, reliable, and intuitive but also transparent, accountable, and secure. Users put their trust in us when they use our products, and I take that responsibility very seriously.

I have over a decade of experience in full-stack software development, devOps, digital marketing, and cybersecurity. I've spent that time building and maintaining complex user-focused applications alongside the healthcare, security, retail, construction, hospitality & real-estate industries.

I've taken small businesses from brand new startup to profitable and built applications with thousands of daily users. I've worked on everything from cutting edge AI tools, to electronic health records which required keeping patient data secure and compliant to strict HIPAA standards.

I'm passionate about technology, always learning, and always looking for my next project. I look forward to working with you.
