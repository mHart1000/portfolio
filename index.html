Sites Built Fully Responsive On Any Device, Any Screen Size.

All sites I create are designed to look great on all devices. There is a common trend among developers, to build sites compatible with common screen sizes, but neglecting the more unusual ones. Meaning those viewing the site on an iPad or Galaxy will have no trouble, but those attempting access on less common phones or tablets, may just see a garbled mess. I take the time to ensure that sites I build look beautiful from the smallest phones, to the largest monitors, and everything in between. In industries where market-research suggests traffic is expected to primarily come from tablets and smart phones, sites will be designed to be mobile-first.
