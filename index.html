I have over a decade of experience in full-stack software development, devOps, digital marketing, and cybersecurity. I've spent that time building and maintaining complex, user-focused applications in the healthcare, security, retail, construction, hospitality & real-estate industries.

I've taken small businesses from brand new startup to profitable, and built applications with thousands of daily users. I've worked on everything from cutting edge AI tools, to messaging and video-chat apps, to electronic health records that require patient data to be secured in compliance with strict HIPAA standards.

I'm passionate about technology, always learning, and always looking for my next project. Let's build something great together.

Computer science is the closest thing we have to magic. We infuse sand with electricity and command it using esoteric symbols to turn our ideas into reality. We have the power to build tools that solve almost any problem, share knowledge across the planet in an instant, and utilize data-driven approaches to making lives better.

But wielding tools this powerful comes with responsibility. We’re building in a world where technology is routinely used for theft, sabotage, propaganda, data-hoarding, and quiet forms of manipulation. With the rise of automation, these threats continue to increase at an exponential rate. Security & awareness in such a world are non-negotiable.

I design systems that collect the minimum data needed, store it safely, and give users real control over how it’s used. Security isn’t just about patching vulnerabilities, it’s about respecting boundaries. I assume that everything I build may someday be attacked or misused, and I plan accordingly.

I ship software that’s not only fast, reliable, and intuitive, but also transparent, accountable, and secure. Users put their trust in us when they use our products, and I take that responsibility very seriously.
